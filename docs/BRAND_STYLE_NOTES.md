# Brand Style Notes

Use bisexual and pansexual pride anchors with strong contrast, human warmth, and clear ownership. Avoid generic SaaS styling. Public visuals should feel community-rooted, strategic, protective, and alive.

Key color anchors: bisexual pink #D60270, bisexual purple #9B4F96, bisexual blue #0038A8, pan pink #FF218C, pan yellow #FFD800, pan blue #21B1FF.
